Our Mission

At FeedHunger.org, we strive to reduce global hunger by providing better agricultural tools, education, and promoting sustainable farming practices. Through the use of HungerCoin, we are working to revolutionize the food industry by taking advantage of trade arbitrage and facilitating donations made in HungerCoin to provide aid where it is needed most. By empowering communities with the tools and resources to feed themselves, we aim to build a future free from hunger.
